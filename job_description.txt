Job Title: Software Engineer – Defense Systems
Company: Apex Defense Technologies
Location: Fort Worth, TX (On-site)

About the Role:
Apex Defense Technologies is seeking a skilled Software Engineer to join our Systems Integration team.
You will design, develop, and maintain software for mission-critical defense applications, working
closely with systems engineers, program managers, and government stakeholders.

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement embedded software and real-time systems for avionics and ground control platforms.
- Develop and maintain C++ and Python codebases for data processing pipelines and simulation tools.
- Integrate software components with hardware systems and conduct unit, integration, and regression testing.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define software requirements and architecture.
- Participate in code reviews, technical documentation, and Agile sprint planning.
- Support debugging and root cause analysis of software defects in deployed systems.
- Ensure compliance with DO-178C, MISRA C++, and other applicable software standards.

Required Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Electrical Engineering, or related field.
- 3+ years of professional software development experience.
- Proficiency in C++ (modern C++11/14/17) and Python.
- Experience with real-time operating systems (RTOS) such as VxWorks or INTEGRITY.
- Strong understanding of software development life cycle (SDLC) and Agile/Scrum methodologies.
- Familiarity with version control systems, particularly Git.
- Active DoD Secret security clearance (or ability to obtain one).

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Model-Based Systems Engineering (MBSE) tools such as MATLAB/Simulink.
- Knowledge of MIL-STD-1553 or ARINC 429 data bus protocols.
- Experience with CI/CD pipelines and DevSecOps practices.
- Familiarity with Linux kernel development or device driver programming.
- Prior experience in an aerospace or defense environment.

Soft Skills:
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
- Ability to work independently and within a team in a fast-paced environment.
- Detail-oriented with a commitment to quality and safety.

Compensation:
- Salary range: $95,000 – $130,000 depending on experience.
- Benefits include health, dental, vision, 401(k) matching, and paid time off.
